## Projects

### AI and the Job Market: News Sentiment Analysis
- **Objective**: Analyze news sentiments regarding AI and the job market.
- **Task**: Scrape 200+ Guardian articles regarding AI and economy since the release of ChatGPT in Novemeber 2022. Use R to perform sentiment analysis examining themes of fear, concern, and hope.
- **Findings**: Analysis revealed peaks in negative sentiment within articles, rather than an overall increasing trend in negativity towards AI.
- See more at https://github.com/felawilliams/ai-and-job-market.

### State of the Union Addresses: Text Analysis
- **Objective**: Identify key themes, such as patriotism and community-building, in U.S. State of the Union Addresses (SOTU) since 1980.
- **Task**: Scrape over 40 SOTU addresses and convert HTML text into Python-readable addresses. Conduct descriptive text analysis to identify themes and sentiments.
- **Findings**: Analysis revealed that there are partisan divides in some speech themes, with Republican presidents calling to patrioti themes more often than Democratic presidents.
- See more at https://github.com/felawilliams/us-sotu-adresses.
